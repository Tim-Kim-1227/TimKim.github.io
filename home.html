Visiting D2 In Two Years

Are you new to NYUAD? Did you just return from the Leave of Absence? Well, I am! School facilities, especially D2, have dramatically changed this semester. “Where did my good-old friend Grills go?” This was my initial thought when I entered renewed D2 for the first time in 2 years. And I am sure that I am not the only one who had faced difficulties in finding food on campus. Menus, and even the counter names, changed, and I had to look through all counters before getting the food. If there has been a video introducing the new D2, I would not have more..
