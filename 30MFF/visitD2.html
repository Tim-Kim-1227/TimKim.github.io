Visiting D2 In Two Years

Are you new to NYUAD? Did you just return from the Leave of Absence? Well, I am! School facilities, especially D2, have dramatically changed this semester. “Where did my good-old friend Grills go?” This was my initial thought when I entered renewed D2 for the first time in 2 years. And I am sure that I am not the only one who had faced difficulties in finding food on campus. Menus, and even the counter names, changed, and I had to look through all counters before getting the food. If there has been a video introducing the new D2, I would not have wasted my time exploring the counters. For this reason, my team created this short virtual tour of D2. If you are still adjusting or new to the school, just watch the video and see which counters fit your palate!

As mentioned previously, the video above is a short video tour of the new D2. The film starts from D2 entrance. As you enter D2, you will see tables, where you can sit and enjoy delicious meal with your friends. If you turn to the right and follow along the hall way, you will start to see counters, where you can order your foods. Be aware that menus vary everyday!
