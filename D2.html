Foods In D2

      VFresh
        VFresh serves vegetarian diet for those who choose not to eat meats. They serve different dishes every day, and you will be surprised how delicious a vegetarian dish can be! Even if you do not like vegetables, the texture and seasoning of the food are right on perfection. They even have vegetarian meats made of beans, which are almost identical to actual meats. If you are on dietary restrictions or want to try something different, VFresh would be a perfect choice!
        Currylicious, as its name suggests, serves rice and curry. If curry is not your go-to menu, do not worry. Here, you have several options in starch and protein. For your starch, you may choose between fried rice and rice. Once you choose your starch, you can choose one protein, which includes curry or a stir-fried dish. Not to mention, Currylicious also has biryani, in which you can have both starch and protein at once. Dishes will change every day but trust me. Regardless of the menu you choose, you will always be satisfied with the quality and taste of the dish.
      Currylicious
      Just Baked
        Just Baked serves pizzas and pastas. Their oven-baked pizzas are always served hot, so do not worry about eating cold and soggy pizzas on this campus! Moreover, you can customize your pasta by choosing vegetables, pasta, and sauces. Once you choose your pasta, a chef will start to cook the pasta right in front of you. The process takes only a few minutes, and the finished dish is surely amazing. If you want an authentic piatto italiano try their pasta and pizza!
        A La Brasa serves grilled meats and deep-fried dishes. Perhaps, A La Brasa is where NYUAD students love the most! They serve numerous menu items, such as chicken and beef steaks, burgers, kebabs, sweet potato fries, etc. Once the order is put, the chefs will begin cooking right in front of you! Though most dishes take some time to be made, the taste is very much worth waiting for. If you are unsure of which food to get, A La Brasa will never fail to satisfy you.
      A La Brasa
      Flavors of The World
        Flavors of The World is very similar to Currylicious as you get to choose one starch and one protein. One unique aspect of the dishes here is that they come from different parts of the world, including Jamaica, Korea, and China. Every day, the theme of the food will change, and this means you can try dishes that you have never tried before. Want something special for todayâ€™s lunch or dinner? Go for Flavors of The World and find foods that intrigue you!
        Subjects serves sandwiches, which you can make your own subs! Here, you get to choose your bread, vegetables, cheese, protein, and sauces! Finding your unique combinations of toppings is one of the most exciting parts of Subjects. Customize your sandwich, and they will toast it for you. Give it 2 minutes to be toasted, and you will be ready to go with delicious and freshly made sub. Subjects is a perfect place to order when you do not have enough time to eat in D2.
      Subjects
      Salad Bar
        NYUAD encourages students to be on healthy diets. Here, you can get a bowl of fresh vegetable salads with some side menus, including hummus, juice, and cakes. Even better, you also get free extra proteins like tuna or boiled eggs on top of your salads! Always, do not forget greens on your plate!
